The next step after a transcript is proofread, is to "Add Tags".
  • SECTION TAGS: These mark the start of a new meeting section. For example: Introduction of Bills, Public Comment, etc. Pre-processing assigns these automatically. You can use the up/down arrows to move their positions.

  • TOPIC TAGS: These name the topics that are being discussed. For example: "Pave Main St.", "Hire policemen", etc. To enter these:
    • Click in the text paragraph to open a dialog OR
    • Highlight a couple of words to use as the topic.
    • You can also select from recent topics.
    • To move a tag, click up/down arrows.
