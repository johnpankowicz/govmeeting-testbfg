Handling county level political entities
It appears that this will probably need to be handled differently. That will depend on the answers to the following questions:
  • Are the meetings open to the public?
  • If so, is the public allowed to speak?
  • What kind of discussion occurs at their meetings?
  • What kind of decisions are made at the county level?
    • The minutes for one of the prior Lincoln County Commissioner meeting shows only decisions made to approve funding levels for county projects. [ See the "View Latest Meeting" box. ]
  • Does the county government have the ability to:
    • Raise taxes?
    • Pass laws binding on the citizens?
